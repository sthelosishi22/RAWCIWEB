Welcome to Restoration & Worship Centre International

We are a vibrant and welcoming church community dedicated to serving our Lord and Savior. At RAWC, we believe in the power of restoration and the grace of God. Our mission is to bring humanity back to Kingship and to be a source of hope, healing, and transformation in the lives of our members and the community.

Join us for worship, fellowship, and spiritual growth as we journey together on the path of faith. Whether you are new to the faith or have been a believer for years, there is a place for you here at Restoration & Worship Centre International.

About Us

Restoration And Worship Centre International is a church that was established in 2009 and is located at Durban 423 Excell House, Smith Street. We believe in restoring humanity back to Kingship and serving the God of restoration. It's a Restoration hour, where we are committed to restoring all that was lost and taking back what belongs to us.

We are a community that believes in being restored, renewed, and rebuilt. We declare that we are kings, born to rule, and we refuse to die before our time. We thank the Lord for restoring joy, peace, our families, healing, finances, and businesses. We shall not die but live, and our mission is to complete the restoration of humanity back to Kingship. Ziyabuya, Sezibuyile - we work and pray.
